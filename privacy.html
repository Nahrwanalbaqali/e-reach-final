Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Bahrain Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 30 of 2018).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our consultation booking services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Booking and consultation details
  • Payment-related information (processed securely by third-party payment providers)

We do not store full payment card details on our servers.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Processing consultation bookings
  • Communicating booking confirmations and updates
  • Processing payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Fulfilling legal and accounting obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Your personal data is processed based on:

  • Your explicit consent
  • The necessity to provide the requested consultation services
  • Compliance with applicable laws in Bahrain

4. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by secure third-party payment providers. We do not store or process full credit or debit card details on our systems.

5. Data Storage and Security

Personal data is stored securely using cloud infrastructure, including services such as Amazon Web Services (AWS). We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may only be shared with:

  • Payment service providers (for payment processing)
  • Service providers necessary to operate the website

All third parties are required to protect personal data in accordance with applicable laws.

7. International Data Transfers

Personal data may be stored or processed outside Bahrain using cloud services. Such transfers are carried out in compliance with Bahrain data protection requirements and with appropriate safeguards.

8. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

9. Your Rights

Under Bahrain Law No. 30 of 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at: Team@ereach.education

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Team@ereach.education
