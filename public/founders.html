Meet Our Founders

E-Reach Consulting was built by international education experts who have walked the path themselves. We are dedicated to making your study abroad journey transparent, ethical, and successful.

Ahmed Adel

Co-Founder & CEO

Ahmed is a Mechanical engineer and a graduate of Bahrain Polytechnic, currently working at the Alba Power Plant. With a strong passion for science and academic life, he founded E.Reach with the belief that students deserve honest guidance from those who have already walked the path. Having experienced the challenges of academic decision-making firsthand, he is committed to building a supportive student-to-student network that helps students make confident and informed choices about their future.

Abdulla Adel

Co-Founder & Director of Admissions

Abdulla is a graduate from Greece and holds an Airline Transport Pilot License (ATPL). With formal aviation training and international academic experience, he has developed a strong understanding of the technical, regulatory, and career pathways within the aviation industry. Through E.Reach, he supports students, especially those interested in aviation and studying abroad by providing structured guidance based on real training experience and industry standards.
