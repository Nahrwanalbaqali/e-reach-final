Refund Policy

At E-Reach, we are committed to providing high-quality academic consulting services. Refunds are handled under the following conditions:

1. Before the Consultation

Clients are eligible for a full refund if the session is cancelled at least 6 hours before the scheduled consultation time.

2. After the Consultation

Once the consultation session has been completed, no refunds will be issued, as the service is considered delivered.

3. Exceptional Cases

In cases where:

  • The consultation was not delivered as scheduled, or
  • A technical issue prevented the session from taking place.

Clients may be eligible for:

  • A full refund, or
  • A rescheduled session, based on their preference.

4. Service Scope

E-Reach provides advisory and guidance services only. We do not guarantee admission to any university or program. Refunds will not be issued based on application outcomes.

5. Contact

For refund requests, please contact us at:
